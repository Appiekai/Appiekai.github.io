WHAT

IS
AIBAVERSE?

Aibaverse is a project created by
Aiba Project Studios, a studio that has been
working in the Solana space since a November
of 2021 in projects such as Jelly Esports,
Sovana, Rugged Revenants, and Solantasy.

Aibaverse is a social simulation game much like Animal Crossing, in Which users are incentivized to
share their designs and play with friends.

DISCORD COMMUNITY
There will be heavy discord connectivity in Aibaverse and
discord is a necessary component for the game. We
understand that communities have built their foundation in
discord and we would like to integrate an experience that
can be shared with projects in the space.
By Engaging with discord, users will be able to drop
furniture and pick up furniture, with a cooldown set on
these bot functions. And these furniture can be used to
build your own spaces.
EDIT FUNCTION
What to do with these
furnitures? With the room
editing function, you are able
to decorate your room to your
liking.
SHOW IT!
Once you have your spaces
built, you can show them off to
your friends in channels where
the bot is present.
PARTNERED NFTS
As an added bonus, projects
that are partnered with us,
such as Jelly Co. which
will be available on launch,
will be able to use their NFT as
NPCs in your room.
Give your friends a friendly
welcome and show them what have
you been up to!
